When a Pakistani company you own shares in earns a profit, it can either
reinvest that profit back into the business or hand a slice of it to
shareholders. That handout is called a **dividend**. For most retail
investors on PSX, dividends are the steadiest way to earn cash from
your shares without having to sell them.

## What you'll learn

- What a dividend is and where the money comes from
- The difference between cash and stock dividends
- The key dates that decide whether you get paid
- How dividends are taxed in Pakistan
