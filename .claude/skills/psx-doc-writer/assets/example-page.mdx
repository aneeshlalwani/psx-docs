If you have ever wondered what happens when a Pakistani company makes a profit, here is one answer. The company can reinvest the profit back into the business, or it can hand a slice of it to shareholders. That handout is called a **dividend**, and for most retail investors on PSX, dividends are the steadiest way to earn cash from your shares without having to sell them.

## What you'll learn

- What a dividend is and where the money comes from
- The difference between cash and stock dividends
- The key dates that decide whether you get paid
- How dividends are taxed in Pakistan
